### deploy-staging.yml — Rewrite

```yaml

  workflow_run:
    workflows: ["CI"]

    branches: [main]

```

### deploy-production.yml — Rewrite

```yaml

    inputs:

        description: 'Git ref to deploy'
        required: false
        default: 'main'
```
Steps: trigger production webhook → wait → verify health at `taskai.cc`
